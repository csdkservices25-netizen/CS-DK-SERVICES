About Us

Building Trust Through Quality and Precision

Your Trusted Construction Partner

CS & DK SERVICES is a leading construction and engineering consultancy firm based in Mirigama, Sri Lanka. We specialize in providing comprehensive Bill of Quantities (BOQ) preparation, construction estimating, and innovative house plan design services.

With over 5 years of industry experience, our team of professional engineers and quantity surveyors is committed to delivering accurate, reliable, and cost-effective solutions for all your construction needs.

Our Mission

To provide precise construction estimates and innovative design solutions that help our clients build their dreams within budget and on time.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in construction estimating and design services in Sri Lanka through excellence and integrity.
